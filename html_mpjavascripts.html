Fuel counter

Scenario: You and Your friends go on a voyage with Your car. Hardly were You able to agree one specific weekend when You all have some free time and now You can go! You don't want to end up like a bunch of math students however, waving around with sheets of paper and pencils on a parking lot in the middle of nowhere, arguing how much each of You should pay for fuel.
How is this useful: Basing upon provided data the program will count how much each member of the expedition should share for fuel.
Disclaimer: Prices of petrol change rapidly. You might want to use this program just before the trip or soon after. Otherwise it's all for nothing.
Source code: This is a second program I wrote in Java Script. Not very complicated actually, but quite useful I think. Unfortunately it does not get the data live from some kind of petrol API - I'm not there yet :). Download this app from my GitHub repository or just hit npm install fuelcounter in Your terminal.

Bird recognizer

Scenario: Normally You just don't pay attention. On Your way to work or home You just think about everything but the diversity and beauty of the world that surrounds You. But there comes a moment when You spot a colorful bird walking on the top of the traffic light and You start wondering what the heck is that.
How is this useful: There are 467 speciecies of birds to be spotted in Poland. In Your surroundings You may spot 10-20 most common species daily. Unless You're an ornithologist You may find it hard to figure out what are You actually looking at. This program will help You!
Disclaimer: Birds are creatures of nature. They may behave beyond our perception and comprehension. This program will not encompass every species, every situation, every circumstance. Do not make bets with Your friends relying solely on the results provided. You may lose and I'm not keen on refunding.
Source code: The most complicated code I've written so far: with arrays, objects, for-loops, callbacks, addEventListeners, querySelectors, some DOM manipulation etc. My God, this was equally depressive and satisfactory trying to put all of these it into motion! Download this app from my GitHub repository or just type npm i birdrecognizer in Your terminal.
